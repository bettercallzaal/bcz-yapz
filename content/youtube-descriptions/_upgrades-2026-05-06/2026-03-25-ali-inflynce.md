BCZ YapZ Episode 13 w/ Ali from Inflynce

In Episode 13 of BCZ YapZ, Zaal sits down with Ali, founder of Inflynce, to break down how one of the earliest Farcaster marketing tools came to life.

Ali shares his journey from trading crypto and losing everything to contributing in a Discord, earning a role through pure effort, and eventually building Inflynce after getting rejected from over 180 job applications. The conversation highlights how real opportunity in Web3 often comes from participation, not credentials.

They dive into the evolution of Inflynce, from a mindshare tracking tool into a full marketing hub that lets builders pay creators directly for engagement. The episode explores how Farcaster changes advertising, why permissionless systems come with tradeoffs, and what it takes to scale from a niche crypto network to broader adoption.

If you are building in Web3, thinking about distribution, or exploring new marketing models, this episode gives a real look at what is working and what is still being figured out.
