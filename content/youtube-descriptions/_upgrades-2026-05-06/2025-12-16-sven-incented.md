BCZ YapZ Episode 6 w/ Sven from Incented

ZM we’re yapping with Sven, founder of Incented - the on-chain protocol redefining how communities coordinate, reward, and create together.

From his early experiments in DAOs to engineering a system for equitable contribution, Sven’s approach to decentralized collaboration blends real-world management principles with Web3’s open ethos.

“I want to live in a world where everyone can work on what they’re most passionate about - and be rewarded equitably for their efforts.”

Together, we dive into the mechanics of human coordination, the balance between incentives and disincentives, and how The ZAO × Incented partnership is pioneering a new way to activate creative communities on-chain.
