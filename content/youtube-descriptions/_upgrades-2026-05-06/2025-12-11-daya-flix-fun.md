BetterCallZaal sits with Daya, co contributor at Flix.Fun, for a detailed look at the new homepage system, the upcoming meDaya node, and the shift from Cosmos to Base.

Daya explains how Flix.Fun evolved from years of work in the Cosmos ecosystem, why creator owned pages matter, and how their new Base deployment enables fast onboarding with email and gasless transactions. The conversation covers live streaming infrastructure, content distribution, video embeds, and what creators gain from hosting pages onchain.

The second half of the episode is a full live demo where Zaal edits his homepage, adds embeds, showcases layouts, and stress tests the new interface in real time. If you build in the creator economy or want tools you can control without platform risk, this episode gives a clear view of what Flix.Fun provides.
